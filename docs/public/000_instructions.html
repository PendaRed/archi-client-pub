Interactive dev documentation for your systems

Edit your design.json to add services, components, databases and dataflows. You can mouse over a service to see a very short tool tip, or click on a service to see a web page dedicated to that service. You can use the data-flow dropdown to display the dataflows. When you select one you get that dataflow web page. You can hover over the arrow in the data flow to see a tool tip for it, or click on the arrow to see the dataflow web page again. It supports a simple z-ordering with 1 being the top and 2 being behind it etc. Services are drawn back to front, and dataflows are always drawn between z-order 2 and 1.
